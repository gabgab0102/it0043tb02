Slowpoke, known as ヤドン (Yadon) in Japanese, is a pink, slow-moving Pokémon that resembles a mix between a hippopotamus and a salamander. It is a dual-type Water/Psychic Pokémon. Slowpoke is famous for its relaxed and oblivious nature.

Eevee

Type:

Normal

Eevee, known as イーブイ (Ībui) in Japanese, is a small, fox-like Pokémon with a distinctive fluffy tail and large ears. It is a Normal-type Pokémon renowned for its ability to evolve into multiple different forms, each representing a different type, such as Vaporeon, Jolteon, and Flareon.
